Overview

Purpose & Audience

This website was built mainly for kids, which explains the typography and the color scheme used. The purpose of a website is to (1) help kids having joy while checking the weather forecast, and (2) provide them advise on how to dress properly.

Personas

My two personas are my children: Rafael, 12 years old, calm boy that loves computer games, and enjoy staying at home; Amanda, 11 years old, adventurous girl, who loves sports and outdoor activities.

Scenarios

What's the weather going to be this weekend?

What should I dress for school today?

How much colder is city A compared to city B?

Will today be a sunny day during my P.E. class?

Will I be warm enough at school just by dressing a t-shirt?
